Found Moments

Making these "Found Moments" videos has quickly become one of my favorite parts of this whole video thing. They originated from extra footage I couldn't use in other projects, but going through it, I felt these moments needed to be shared. Once I made the first compilation, they just kind of kept happening. I like to think they tell the story behind the story.
