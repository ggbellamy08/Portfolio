About Griff

Hey! I’m Griff. I love hats, cameras, and doing new things. My video journey started in the dorms of the University of Nebraska making skits with my roommates. Since then, I’ve been working with brands in Lincoln while also saving time for some personal projects! Outside of video, I’m pursuing a master’s degree in Educational Psychology and also help coach cross country at a high school in town!

My Process

Before I start any project with a brand, I want to get to know you! I want to know your story inside and out so I can share it with a larger audience. This most often has taken the form of a consultation where we discuss ideas, however I would love to immerse myself in your world however you see fit! When I have an idea of how best to tell your story, I get to work on making shot lists, story boards, and whatever else comes to mind. Once we’re both excited about our concept, all that’s left to do is start shooting! After the shoot, I always give a preview of a project to get feedback. I believe collaboration is not only fun, but essential in video work. After all it is your project as much as mine! I’ll make the desired changes and then you’ll receive the final product. After that, I’ll look forward to the next time we work together!

My Ideal Client

My dream client is someone who is passionate about what they do. Whether it be a business, organization, project, or hobby, I love working with people who are excited about things. Not only does it make for a fun time, it makes my job easier. Without a doubt, audiences can feel genuine passion and latch on whenever it's present. Additionally, your passion will spark new ideas that I may have never considered. There's nothing more satisfying than thinking of a new angle to approach a project in a client meeting. If this sounds like you, then don't hesitate to reach out!
